Welcome

Welcome to the Entropic Visualizer, an online web software that uses entropy graphs to analyze
RNA and DNA sequences.

What are Entropy Graphs?

Entropy is the measure of disorder in a data set. By using software, one can analyze the entropy
in a data set and display the result in a graph. These graphs are called entropy graphs.

What is the Entropic Visualizer's Purpose?

The Entropic Visualizer was created in order to anaylize differences and similarites in various
RNA or DNA sequences. Our hope is that the Entropic Visualizer will be used to aid those in the
medical field to develope vaccines for COVID-19.
